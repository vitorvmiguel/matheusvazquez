About me

Fueled by the echoes of his father’s old rock and MPB (Brazilian Popular Music) CDs, Matheus found purpose the moment he first sat behind a drum kit—and never looked back. His passion for drums surpasses the barriers of music styles, throughout a career that highlights a varied sonic palette, he helped bring countless projects to life both recording and touring in some of the greatest studios and venues in Brazil. Now, he gifts the world with his own compositions: a bold mixture of progressive rock, jazz fusion, and the unmistakable Brazilian syncope. Known for his powerful and captivating live performances, he is gentle but loud and brings a unique rhythmic approach to the stage, layering complex grooves with electrifying soundscapes captured from contemporary instrumentation to create something closer to hallucination—and reach the audience’s deepest emotions.

Contact me

Throughout my career I’ve worked with a wide range of artists accumulating over 20 collaborations recording and touring. As a mentor I’ve helped countless students to find their own path on the journey of learning drums, I’ve been teaching drums for 10 years now and gathered experience from different schools, private lessons and with an online course that gathered over 40 students during the pandemic.
